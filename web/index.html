The translation is drawn right where you're reading.

Capture

Grab any text on screen.

Drag a region or pick a whole window. The translation lands over a blurred copy of the original.

✂️

Region or window

Drag to select any area, or press Space to highlight and click a whole window, like the macOS screenshot tool.

🖼️

Translated preview

The result opens in a floating window with each line blurred behind its translation.

💾

Save or copy

Save a PNG, copy the image, or copy just the original or translated text.

Live overlay

Translation that keeps up.

Place an overlay on a region or window and it keeps translating as the content changes.

🪟

Snaps into place

Pick a region or a window and the overlay lands right on it, translating live from the first frame. It remembers that spot, so a shortcut can bring it back later without re-selecting.

👆

Stays out of the way

Clicks and scrolling pass straight through to the app below. Move it by the top-left handle and resize from the edges; the LIVE badge pauses and resumes, and × closes it.

↔️

In place or in a window

Draw the translation over the source, or send it to a separate window while the overlay stays a thin frame.

Recognition

It reads the way you do.

Recognition follows the page's structure, so even busy and vertical layouts come out right.

📑

Reads in order

Groups text into reading-order paragraphs, so comics and multi-column pages don't get scrambled.

↕️

Vertical, in place

Vertical Japanese and Chinese stay vertical, with the translation drawn over the original.

🔎

Clean on busy pages

Separates ruby (furigana) from the body text it annotates and handles dense layouts without jumbling lines.

Private by design

No cloud. No keys. No quotas.

Recognition and translation run on-device with Apple's Vision and Translation frameworks. Nothing leaves your Mac.

🔒

Fully on-device

Powered by Apple's on-device models. Works offline, with nothing sent to a server.

🌐

Your Mac's languages

Source and target lists come from the languages installed on your Mac. Set the source to Auto to detect it per line.

⚡

Instant re-captures

Translating the same screen again is served from cache, so repeat captures are immediate.

Always at hand

Lives in your menu bar.

No Dock icon, no clutter. Trigger everything with a shortcut and keep it current automatically.

🐦‍⬛

Menu bar agent

Open the popover or fire any action from a global shortcut, even with no window open.

⌨️

Your shortcuts

Bind capture, live overlay, show/hide overlay on the last region, pause/resume, display mode, and the save/copy keys in Settings.

🔄

Updates itself

Sparkle keeps SwiftyCrow up to date, and it can launch at login if you want.

Get SwiftyCrow.

Free, open source, and lives in your menu bar.
